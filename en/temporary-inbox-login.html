MailCatch Premium Features

What do I get?

Premium MailCatch users will get access to two extra features:

  • Private MailCatch Host: You will gain your very own private MailCatch domain. All emails sent there will only be readable by you and since this is a new domain name websites are unlikely to ban you from using it.
  • Email forwarding: For any email received by MailCatch, be it on mailcatch.com or your private host, you can setup a timed automatic forward, so that you get new mails delivered to your real, hidden, email account

Why do I have to pay?

First and foremost, registering a domain name costs money; so does doing it automatically and without any hassle (barring some waiting time) for you.
